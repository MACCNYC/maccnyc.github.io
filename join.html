Attending a monthly Care Assembly is a great way to get connected to MACC working groups and projects!

Check our calendar for upcoming Care Assemblies.

Upcoming Events

Can't make a Care Assembly? See our calendar for other upcoming events like our book club, demos, socials, or open working group meetings.

Other ways to get involved

MACC is open to all anarchists and those interested in anarchists ideas such as direct action, mutual aid, direct democracy, anti-oppression, and horizontalism. To learn more about how MACC operates, check out our core documents.

You can also follow us on Twitter, Instagram, or Mastodon to get updates about other MACC projects and events.
